Procedure

Step-by-step description of metholodology used in this study


Step

Description

Image

1

Purchase and Download EmotivPRO

EmotivPRO is the software needed to record and analyze EEG data

2

Prepare EEG Headset

In order to prepare the EEG, the sensors must be hydrated with saline solution

3

Connect EEG to EmotivPRO

Connection can be wired or wireless via Bluetooth

4

Fit Headset to Participant

Each sensor should be contacting the participant's skin for best reading

5

Check Sensor Quality Map

EEG quality should be 80% or higher; if this is not the case, rehydrate sensors

6

Record Baseline Data

A 30 second recording must be taken of the participant relaxed with their eyes open

7

Record Experimental Data

During the experimental portion of the recording, participants will be read a (4-5 digit)
string of numbers, which they will be asked to repeat backwards (ex. 1234 becomes 4321)
